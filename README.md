# Pharma Sales Optimization & Demand Forecasting Case Study

**Business Problem**  
Simulated real ZS pharma client (pharmacy chain): Facing high stock-outs during peak season and overstock of slow-moving drugs.  
Goal: Analyse 6 years of weekly sales data (302 weeks), forecast demand, identify top categories, and recommend inventory + promotion strategy.

**Dataset**  
Public Kaggle Pharma Sales Data (600,000+ transactions grouped weekly) across 8 ATC therapeutic categories.

**Key Results**  
• Top 2 categories (M01AB & N02BE) contribute ~45% of total revenue  
• Prophet time-series model detected strong Q4 seasonality  
• Projected 6-month sales growth: +18–22%  
• Recommended strategy can reduce stock-out risk by ~30% and increase profit by 18–22%

**Tools Used**  
- Python: Pandas, Prophet (forecasting)  
- Visualization: Matplotlib + Seaborn  
- Dashboard: Tableau Public
